About Me

I am a Research Associate at Information Processing and Communications Lab at Imperial College London working on Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning with Prof. Deniz Gunduz. I have completed my PhD in Computer Science at Queen Mary University of London as a member of Centre for Intelligent Sensing under the supervision of Prof. Andrea Cavallaro (formerly, Dr. Hamed Haddadi) and Dr. Richard G. Clegg, supported by Life Sciences Institute. My Ph.D. research was focused on Machine Learning Algorithms for Privacy-preserving Behavioral Data Analytics, particularly for motion sensor data generated by mobile and wearable devices. I have been a Ph.D. intern at Brave Software where I worked on privacy-preserving content personalization, working with Dr. Dimitrios Athanasakis and Dr. Benjamin Livshits. I have also been a visiting PhD student at Imperial College London, as a member of DataboxProject, working with Dr. Hamed Haddadi. Please download my CV for more information.
